About P-Chessbot

The story behind the engine.

Our Mission

P-Chessbot was created as a learning-friendly, open-source chess engine written in Python. The project’s mission is to bring a balance of strength (~2000 Elo) and simplicity, so developers and chess lovers can both enjoy and improve it.

Why Python?

Python is accessible and easy to learn, making it the perfect language for new programmers. While it may not be the fastest, it allows developers to understand chess programming concepts like evaluation, search, and move generation without complex optimization.

Community Driven

P-Chessbot is a community effort. Developers, testers, and chess players contribute by:

  • Improving the evaluation and move ordering.
  • Testing against other engines and players.
  • Writing documentation and tutorials.
  • Sharing feedback to improve usability.
